Interculturality:

Describes a set of multi-faceted processes of interaction through which relations between different cultures are constructed, aiming to enable groups and individuals to forge links between cultures based on equity and mutual respect.

Ethnocentrism:

The concept of evaluating other cultures according to preconceptions originating in the standards of one's own culture.
