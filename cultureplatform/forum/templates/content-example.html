TOPIC: IS IT ETHICAL TO EAT MEAT?
Some people argue that animals have a distinctly different moral standing from humans. Eating an animal in the same bucket as cutting a flower from his garden, explaining that “although animals experience pain as it is physically bad, their experience of it is not in itself morally bad. They are harmed in feeling pain, but this harm is not of a moral kind … Since animals lack moral status, it is not wrong to eat meat, even if this is not essential to nutrition.” Others believe that the pain and suffering animals endure is not similar to was is felt by humans and thus making it ethical to eat meat for sustenance.
